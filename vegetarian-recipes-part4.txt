RECIPE 70: Flax Seed Laddoo
========================================================================
URL: https://www.sanjeevkapoor.com/Recipe/Flax-Seed-Laddoo.html

Powerhouse of nutrition, you can enjoy these laddoos without feeling guilty This is a Sanjeev Kapoor exclusive recipe.

Main Ingredients: Flax seeds, Jaggery | Cuisine: Indian | Course: Mithais | Prep Time: 21-25 minutes | Cook Time: 21-25 minutes | Serve: 4 | Taste: Sweet | Level of Cooking: Moderate | Diet: Veg

INGREDIENTS:
  - 1/2 cups Jaggery , chopped
  - 1/2 cup Raw peanuts
  - 1/2 cup Almonds
  - 1/2 cup Dried coconut , grated
  - 1/2 cup Oats
  - 1/2 cup White sesame seeds
  - 2 tablespoons Black sesame seeds
  - 2 tablespoons Garden cress (alive) seeds
  - 1 teaspoon Green cardamom powder
  - 1 tablespoon Ghee

METHOD:
  1. Heat a non-stick pan, add jaggery and ¼ cup water, cook, stirring, till a syrup of one string consistency is formed.
  2. Heat another non-stick pan, add flax seeds and dry roast on medium heat for 1-2 minutes and set aside on a plate to cool down.
  3. In the same pan dry roast raw peanuts on medium heat for 2-3 minutes, set aside with flax seeds.
  4. Dry roast almonds, on medium low heat, for 2-3 minutes, keep along with flax seeds mixture.
  5. Dry roast dried coconut and oats for 1-2 minutes and set aside along with the flax seed mixture, let it cool down completely.
  6. Further dry roast white sesame seeds, black sesame seeds and aliv together for 1-2 minutes, set aside in a bowl.
  7. Grind together the flax seed mixture to a coarse powder.
  8. Transfer into a bowl, add the sesame seeds mixture and jaggery syrup and mix well Divide the mixture into equal portions and shape each portion into a laddoo and set aside on a plate to cool down to room temperature.
  9. Once cooled completely store in an airtight container or serve.

RECIPE 71: Fresh Spring Roll Wrappers
========================================================================

INGREDIENTS:
  - 1½ cups boiled and shredded chicken
  - 1½ tablespoons finely chopped garlic
  - 1 spring onion, chopped with the greens
  - 1 small carrot, cut into thin strips
  - 150 grams shredded cabbage
  - 1 cup mixed coloured capsicum strips
  - 1 teaspoon vinegar
  - 1 teaspoon dark soy sauce Salt to taste Crushed black peppercorns to taste Refined flour slurry for applying Sliced spring onion greens for garnish Schezwan sauce to serve

METHOD:
  1. Take refined flour in a large bowl.
  2. Add salt and 1¼ cups water little by little and mix with a wooden spoon till a smooth batter is formed.
  3. Continue to mix for 5 minutes.
  4. Cover and set aside to rest for 1½ hours.
  5. To make the stuffing, heat oil in a non-stick wok.
  6. Add garlic and ginger and sauté for a minute.
  7. Add spring onion bulb and sauté for a few seconds.
  8. Add carrot and cabbage and mix well.
  9. Sauté for 1-2 minutes.
  10. Add coloured capsicum strips, vinegar, dark soy sauce, salt and crushed black peppercorns and mix well.
  11. Add chicken and mix well.
  12. Add spring onion greens and toss.
  13. Take the pan off the heat and allow the mixture to cool.Mix the rested batter well with your hand for 1-2 minutes.
  14. Heat a non-stick tawa¬ on low heat.
  15. Take a big portion of the dough and spread a thin layer of the batter in a circular motion and collect the excess dough and keep it aside.
  16. Cook for 10-15 seconds, flip and cook for 1-2 seconds.
  17. Remove from heat and set aside.
  18. Similarly, make the remaining spring roll wrappers.
  19. Use as required.
  20. To make spring rolls, place a spring roll sheet on a worktop, place a generous portion of the stuffing in the centre.
  21. Apply some refined flour slurry on the edges, fold the sides and tightly roll it.
  22. Heat sufficient oil in a kadai.
  23. Gently slide in the rolls a few at a time and deep fry till golden brown and crisp.
