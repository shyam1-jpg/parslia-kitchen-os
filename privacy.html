Privacy Policy

Last updated: 20 July 2026 · Applies to parslia.app and Parslia Kitchen OS early access.

Who we are

Parslia Kitchen OS (“Parslia”, “we”, “us”) provides kitchen operations software for professional food businesses. Contact: hello@parslia.app.

What information we collect

  • Early access enquiries — name, email, company, role, business type, and the details you submit in the form.
  • Account data (when the app is live) — login email, kitchen profile, team roles and permissions.
  • Kitchen operational data — recipes, menus, allergens, stock, logs, rota and related records you enter.
  • Technical data — essential cookies for sign-in and basic site function. We do not load advertising cookies.

Why we process information

  • To respond to early access and demo requests.
  • To provide and improve the Parslia Kitchen OS service.
  • To keep kitchens secure (access control, audit trail when those features are live).
  • To meet legal obligations and App Store / Play Store requirements.

Legal basis

Where UK GDPR applies, we rely on: your consent (enquiries and optional marketing); contract (providing the service you sign up for); and legitimate interests (securing and improving the product) where appropriate.

How long we keep data

Early access enquiries are kept while we are onboarding kitchens, then deleted or anonymised when no longer needed. Account and kitchen data are retained while your organisation uses Parslia, and for a limited period after closure as needed for legal or security reasons. You can request deletion — see Delete account.

Who we share with

We do not sell personal data. We may use trusted infrastructure providers (hosting, email delivery) under contract. We share data only when required by law or to provide the service you requested.

Where data is stored

Customer information will be stored with reputable cloud hosts. Exact regions will be confirmed in product documentation before general launch. Until then, early access enquiries are received by email at hello@parslia.app.

Security

See our Security overview. We use HTTPS on this site. Additional controls (encryption at rest, backups, staff PIN, audit trail) will be described honestly as each capability goes live.

Your rights

Depending on your location you may request access, correction, deletion, restriction, or portability of your personal data, and object to certain processing. Email hello@parslia.app. You may also complain to the UK Information Commissioner’s Office (ICO).

Children

Parslia is for professional kitchen businesses, not for children.

Changes

We will update this page when our practices change and refresh the date above.
