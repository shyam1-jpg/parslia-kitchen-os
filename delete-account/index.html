Account

Delete your account

Required for App Store and Google Play account-based apps.

How to request deletion

  1. Email hello@parslia.app from the address linked to your account.
  2. Use the subject line: Delete Parslia account.
  3. Include your kitchen / organisation name and confirm you want permanent deletion.

What we delete

On verification we remove account login details and associated kitchen operational data held for your organisation, unless we must retain a limited record for legal, security or billing reasons.

Timing

We aim to complete verified deletion requests within 30 days. You will receive an email confirmation when the process is finished.

In-app deletion

When the Parslia app launches with accounts, an in-app path to initiate deletion will be available alongside this public page.

Early access enquiries only

If you only submitted the early access form and never created an account, email us to ask that your enquiry be removed from our list.
