RECIPE 93: Jain Pakoda
========================================================================
URL: https://www.sanjeevkapoor.com/Recipe/jain-pakoda-7090850

Simple and delicious pakodas made with cabbage and gram flour. Simply irresistible ! In this Jain version, onions are replaced with cabbage . This is Sanjeev Kapoor exclusive recipe.

Cuisine: Indian | Course: Snacks and Starters Prep time 10-15 minutes | Cook Time: 10-15 minutes | Serve: 4 | Taste: Mild Level of cooking Easy | Diet: Vegetarian

INGREDIENTS:
  - 1 small cabbage, thinly shredded
  - 1 cup gram flour (besan)
  - 2 tablespoons rice flour
  - 2 tablespoons chopped fresh coriander leaves
  - 1 teaspoon sugar ¼ teaspoon asafoetida (hing)
  - 1 teaspoon red chilli powder ½ teaspoon crushed black peppercorns
  - 1 teaspoon lemon juice Salt to taste
  - 2 teaspoons oil + for deep frying Jain green chutney to serve

METHOD:
  1. Take cabbage in a large bowl.
  2. Add gram flour, rice flour, coriander leaves, sugar, asafoetida, red chilli powder, crushed black peppercorns, lemon juice, salt, and oil and mix well.
  3. Add ½ cup water and mix till a thick mixture is formed.

  5. Gently slide in small portions of the mixture and deep fry till golden brown and crisp.

  7. Arrange the pakodas on a serving plate and serve hot with Jain green chutney.

RECIPE 94: Jain Seekh Kebab
========================================================================
URL: https://www.sanjeevkapoor.com/Recipe/jain-seekh-kebab-7092314

Finger food gets a Jain twist. The joy of a pure veg seekh kebab with veggies and raisins that are very succulent. This is Sanjeev Kapoor exclusive recipe.

Cuisine: Indian | Course: Snacks and Starters Prep time 15-20 minutes | Cook Time: 10-15 minutes | Serve: 4 | Taste: Mild Level of cooking Moderate | Diet: Vegetarian

INGREDIENTS:
  - 3-4 medium raw bananas, boiled, peeled and mashed
  - 4 tablespoons oil
  - 2-3 green chillies, finely chopped ½ cup finely chopped cabbage ¼ teaspoon turmeric powder
  - 4-5 raisins (kismis)
  - 2 tablespoons chopped fresh coriander leaves
  - 1 tablespoons finely chopped fresh mint leaves
  - 2 tablespoons roasted gram flour Cabbage salad to serve Jain green chutney to serve Fresh mint leaves for garnish Lemon wedges to serve

METHOD:
  1. Heat 1 tbsp oil in a non-stick pan.
  2. Add green chillies and cabbage and mix well.
  3. Add turmeric powder and mix well.
  4. Cook for 1 minute.
