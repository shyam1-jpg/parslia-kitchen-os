FAQ

Questions kitchens ask before joining.

Can Parslia work without internet?

Core hosted features need a connection. Offline-friendly workflows for the pass are on the roadmap and will be labelled when available.

Can I import existing recipes?

Import tools are planned for early access kitchens. Tell us your format (CSV, Excel, PDF) when you request access.

Does it calculate food costs?

Food costing is part of the stock and recipe roadmap. Early access kitchens help prioritise what ships first.

Does it identify all 14 UK allergens?

Allergen control is designed around the 14 UK allergens. Always verify final guest-facing information in your own kitchen process.

Can one account manage several kitchens?

Multi-site support is planned for Professional and Enterprise kitchens.

Can staff have different permissions?

Manager and staff roles are part of the security model — see Security above.

Can I use my existing Bluetooth probes?

Probe integrations are planned. Share your device model when you book a demo.

Can Parslia print food labels?

Label printing is on the roadmap, including common kitchen printers such as Sunmi.

Is there an iPhone and Android app?

iOS and Android builds are being prepared for store review. Join early access for the download link when live. Badges on this site currently lead to early access — not a store download yet.

Can I export my data?

CSV, Excel and PDF exports are planned so you can leave with your records.

How are backups handled?

Automatic backups will apply to hosted workspaces at app launch. Details will be published in Security and Privacy.

Can I cancel and delete my account?

Yes. Use Delete account or email hello@parslia.app. In-app deletion will ship with accounts.
