ChatGPT + Kiteline

Add Kiteline to ChatGPT

Kiteline is a multipurpose business and hospitality platform for hotels, restaurants, catering companies, commercial kitchens, schools and colleges, care homes, retreat centres, cafés, bakeries, event venues and other food businesses. ChatGPT connects to your company only — never another customer’s data.

What ChatGPT can do

  • Search recipes, products and dishes
  • Create and manage menus
  • Search stock and suppliers
  • Generate shopping and ordering lists
  • Read and add temperature records
  • Generate allergen and nutrition reports
  • Search staff rotas and operational records
  • Produce business, cost and compliance reports

Dietary rules stay yours

Vegetarian, vegan, Jain, Ekadashi, halal, kosher, gluten-free and similar rules are configurable per business in Settings. They are not permanently applied to every Kiteline customer. If your company has not enabled a diet rule, ChatGPT will not assume one.

Connect in 4 steps

  1. Sign in to Kiteline as a company Admin → Settings → Connect ChatGPT.
  2. Create an AI token (or use OAuth when enabled). Never share your login password.
  3. In ChatGPT, create a Custom GPT → Actions → import schema:
    https://kiteline.uk/api/ai/openapi.json
  4. Set authentication to Bearer / API Key with your kl_ai_… token, or OAuth from the same Settings panel.

MCP discovery endpoint: https://kiteline.uk/mcp (JSON-RPC tools for compatible clients).

Security

  • Each AI token is bound to one company workspace and optional site list
  • Fine-grained permissions (recipes, temps, stock, reports, etc.)
  • Write actions require explicit confirm: true after you approve the change
  • Audit log of AI actions in your Kiteline workspace
