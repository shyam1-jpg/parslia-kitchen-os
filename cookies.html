Cookie Policy

Last updated: 20 July 2026

Summary

Parslia uses essential cookies and local storage only. We do not load advertising or third-party analytics cookies on this marketing site today.

What we use

  • Essential session cookies — to keep you signed in when the Kitchen OS app is available.
  • Local preference storage — for example remembering cookie consent or onboarding progress in the browser.

What we do not use

We do not currently use advertising cookies, social trackers, or third-party marketing pixels on parslia.app.

Managing cookies

You can clear cookies and site data in your browser settings. Blocking essential cookies may prevent sign-in when the app launches.
