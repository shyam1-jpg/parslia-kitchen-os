Refund Policy

Kiteline Academy is operated in the UK.

One-time course purchases

If you have not accessed more than one paid lesson, you may request a refund within 14 days of purchase by emailing contact@kiteline.uk.

Monthly subscriptions

You may cancel anytime. Access continues until the end of the current billing period. We do not refund partial months unless required by law.

Annual bundle (£99)

Includes 12 months access from purchase date. Refunds within 14 days if fewer than 2 course modules have been completed.

How to request

Email contact@kiteline.uk with your account email and purchase details. We respond within 5 business days.
