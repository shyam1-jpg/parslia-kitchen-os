    Frequently asked questions

    Do I need coding experience?

    No. Beginner courses start from zero. We explain every step in plain language.

    Can I learn from mobile?

    Yes. Lessons and the student dashboard are mobile-friendly. Code Lab works best on a laptop or tablet.

    Do I get a certificate?

    Yes — after completing all lessons and quizzes in a course. Certificates show completion only; they are not formal accredited qualifications unless clearly stated.

    How do payments work?

    Create a free account first. Paid plans use secure Stripe checkout when enabled. You can also email contact@kiteline.uk for help. See our refund policy.

    Can I cancel my subscription?

    Yes. Monthly Starter and Pro plans can be cancelled anytime before your next billing date from your student dashboard Billing tab. See our refund policy.

    What is your refund policy?

    Digital content access starts immediately. UK consumers may lose the 14-day cancellation right once streaming begins — you confirm this at checkout. See refunds.html for full details.

    Is there a minimum age?

    Accounts are for learners aged 13+. Under-18s should have a parent or guardian’s permission.

    I forgot my password or cannot sign in

    Use Forgot password? on the sign-in form. If email does not arrive, click Resend verification — we also show a verify link on screen. Check spam/junk.

    How do I delete my account?

    Email contact@kiteline.uk from your registered address and we will delete your account and personal data within 30 days.

    Are payments secure?

    Yes. Card payments are processed through Stripe. We never store your full card number.

    Do you use analytics cookies?

    We use essential session and language cookies only. If we add analytics or marketing cookies later, we will show a cookie choice banner. See cookie policy.

    Where are your policies?

    Read our terms, privacy policy, refund policy and cookie policy.
