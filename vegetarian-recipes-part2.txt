  - 2½ cups soya bean flour Salt to taste
  - 4 tablespoons ghee
  - 1 teaspoon carom seeds (ajwain)
  - 1 cup black eyed beans (lobia) Filling
  - 1 cup yogurt Salt to taste A pinch of black salt ½ teaspoon chaat masala
  - 2 tablespoons powdered sugar ¼ cup roasted peanuts, peeled
  - 1 cup dried white peas (matara), soaked overnight, boiled with salt and turmeric powder
  - 2 medium sweet potatoes, boiled, peeled and cut into ½ inch cubes

  - 1 medium beetroot, peeled and grated Chaat masala to sprinkle Tamarind chutney to drizzle Green chutney to drizzle Nylon sev for garnish Blanched carrot, cut into star shaped pieces for garnish Blanched beetroot, cut into star shaped pieces for garnish

  11. Add sapodilla puree and mix well.
  12. Transfer into a tall jug.
  13. Pour the mixture into metal kulfi moulds till they are ¾ full.
  14. Cover them with their lids and keep the moulds in the freezer for 2 hours or till semi set.

  - 1/4 teaspoon Baking soda
  - 1/4 teaspoon Green cardamom powder to deep fry oIL
  - 1 tablespoon Milk to garnish Pistachio slivers

METHOD:
