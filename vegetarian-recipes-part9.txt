RECIPE 185: Saeb Makhane ki Kheer
========================================================================
URL: https://www.sanjeevkapoor.com/Recipe/Saeb-Makhane-ki-Kheer-Turban-Tadka-FoodFood.html

Makhana sautéed in ghee enhances the taste and falvour of this kheer This recipe is from FoodFood TV channel

Main Ingredients: Medium apples (saeb), Lotus seeds (makhana) | Cuisine: Indian | Course: Mithais | Prep Time: 6-10 minutes | Cook Time: 31-40 minutes | Serve: Taste Sweet | Taste: Sweet | Level of Cooking: Easy | Diet: Veg

INGREDIENTS:
  - 2 medium Medium apples (saeb) , grated
  - 1 cup Lotus seeds (makhana)
  - 1/4 cup Ghee
  - 1/4 cup Jaggery , grated
  - 1/4 teaspoon Green cardamom powder
  - 500 millilitres Milk
  - 1 cup Khotya (mawa) , grated to garnish Apple fans

METHOD:
  1. Heat ghee in a non-stick pan, add makhana and sauté till crisp.
  2. Drain and transfer into a bowl and set aside to cool.
  3. Add apples to the ghee remaining in the pan and sauté for 7-8 minutes.
  4. Transfer makhana into a grinder jar and grind to a fine powder.
  5. Add jaggery to the apples and mix till it melts.
  6. Add green cardamom powder and mix well.
  7. Transfer the mixture into another bowl.
  8. Add nuts to the same pan and roast till lightly browned.
  9. Add milk and bring to a boil.
  10. Add makhana powder, mix well and cook till the mixture thickens.
  11. Add khoya and mix till it melts.
  12. Switch off heat, add prepared apple mixture and mix till well combined.
  13. Transfer into a serving bowl, garnish with apple fans and serve hot.

RECIPE 186: Sambousek
========================================================================

A delightful Middle Eastern pastry filled with with spicy chenna and spinach mixture, inviting you to savor its crispy exterior and flavorful fillings in every bite. This recipe is from FoodFood TV channel

RECIPE 187: Samosa Patti
========================================================================
URL: https://www.sanjeevkapoor.com/Recipe/Samosa-Patti.html

Samosa patti are broad strips made of wheat or refined flour which serve as an outer covering for the stuffing of samosas This is a Sanjeev Kapoor exclusive recipe.

Main Ingredients: Refined flour (maida), Oil | Cuisine: Indian | Course: Snacks and Starters | Prep Time: 31-40 minutes | Cook Time: 6-10 minutes | Serve: 4 | Taste: Mild | Level of Cooking: Moderate | Diet: Veg

INGREDIENTS:
  - 1 cup refined flour (maida) + for dusting
  - 3 teaspoons oil + for applying Salt to taste

METHOD:
  1. Heat oil in a small pan.
  2. Take refined flour in a large bowl.
  3. Add salt and the hot oil and mix till the mixture comes together.
  4. Add ¼ cup water little by little and knead till a soft dough is formed.
  5. Cover with a damp muslin cloth and set aside to rest for 30 minutes.
  6. Divide the dough into 6 equal portions.
  7. Shape each portion into a ball.
  8. Place a dough ball on a worktop and dust with some refined flour.
  9. Roll into a small disc.
  10. Apply some oil and generously dust some refined flour over it.
  11. Place this on a plate.
  12. Dust the worktop with some refined flour again and place another dough ball on it.
  13. Roll it into a small disc.
  14. Repeat this process one more time with another dough ball.
  15. Place the first disc over the second one with the flour side facing each other.
  16. Similarly, place the third disc with the flour side facing down over the sandwiched discs.
  17. Dust some flour on top and roll it into a large thin disc.
  18. Heat a non-stick tawa.
  19. Place the rolled out discs and cook on low heat for a few minutes while rotating the tawa to ensure the discs are uniformly semi-cooked.
  20. Flip and cook the other side for a few minutes as well.
  21. Carefully separate the layers and place a worktop.
  22. Trim the sides and cut into 24cm x 8cm sized thick strips.
  23. Carefully place them in a zip lock bag and keep them in the freezer.
  24. Use as required.

Momos stuffed with sweet coconut mixture and cooked in This recipe is from FoodFood TV channel

  - 1/2 cups for dusting Refined flour (maida) for applyi tablespoons Ghee a pinch Salt
  - 4 tablespoons Sugar
  - 1 tablespo to garnish Mixed nuts (almonds and pistachios) , chopped
  - 1 cup Fresh coconut , scraped
  - 1 cup Jaggery , chopped

METHOD:
  1. Take refined flour in a bowl.
  2. Add 1 tbsp ghee, salt, 1 tbsp sugar, 3 tbsps coconut milk and a pinch of green cardamom powder and mix well.
  3. Add sufficient water and knead to medium stiff dough.
  4. Apply ghee on the dough, cover with muslin cloth and set aside to rest.
  5. To make filling, heat remaining ghee in a stainless steel pan, add mixed nuts and coconut and sauté lightly.
