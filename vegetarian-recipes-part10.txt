RECIPE 208: Taazi Haldi aur Gajar ki Sabzi
========================================================================
URL: https://www.sanjeevkapoor.com/Recipe/Taazi-Haldi-aur-Gajar-ki-Sabzi.html

A unique dish made with fresh turmeric and carrot which is luscious and easy to make. Fresh turmeric and carrots cooked with Indian spices and served. This is a Sanjeev Kapoor exclusive recipe.

Main Ingredients: Fresh turmeric (taazi haldi), Carrot | Cuisine: Indian | Course: Main | Prep Time: 11-15 minutes | Cook Time: 21-25 minutes | Serve: 4 | Taste: Spicy | Level of Cooking: Easy | Diet: Veg

INGREDIENTS:
  - 1/2 cups fresh turmeric (taazi haldi) , chopped
  - 1 medium carrot , chopped
  - 2-3 tablespoons ghee
  - 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  - 1/4 teaspoon asafoetida
  - 2 tablespoons crushed ginger-garlic-green chillies
  - 1 large onion , chopped Salt to taste
  - 1/4 cup yogurt
  - 1 teaspoon red chilli powder
  - 1/2 tablespoon cumin powder
  - 1 tablespoon coriander powder
  - 5-6 tablespoons fresh coriander leaves , chopped

METHOD:
  1. Heat ghee in a non-stick kadai, add cumin seeds and saute till they change colour.
  2. Add asafoetida and crushed ginger-garlic-green chillies, mix and saute for 1-2 minutes.
  3. Add onion, mix and saute till translucent.
  4. Add fresh turmeric and carrot, mix well and saute for 2-3 minutes.
  5. Add salt, mix, cover and cook on medium heat for 6-8 minutes.
  6. Add yogurt, red chilli powder, cumin powder, coriander powder, a little salt and 3 tbsps coriander leaves and mix well.
  7. Cover and cook for 2-3 minutes or till ghee surfaces.
  8. Add remaining coriander leaves and mix well.
  9. Transfer the sabzi into a serving bowl, garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot.

RECIPE 224: Zarda Boondi Biryani
========================================================================
URL: https://www.sanjeevkapoor.com/Recipe/Zarda-Boondi-Biryani-Turban-Tadka-FoodFood.html

Sweet biryani made with sweet boondi, basmati rice and plenty of nuts This recipe is from FoodFood TV channel

Main Ingredients: saffron strands, Sweet boondi | Cuisine: Indian | Course: Rice | Prep Time: 26-30 minutes | Cook Time: 16-20 minutes | Serve: 4 | Taste: Sweet | Level of Cooking: Easy | Diet: Veg

INGREDIENTS:
  - 1/2 grams saffron strands
  - 1 cup Sweet boondi
  - 3 cups Basmati rice , cooked
  - 2 tablespoons Ghee
  - 2 tablespoons Chironjee / charoli
  - 2-3 tablespoons Cashewnuts , chopped
  - 1/2 cup Milk
  - 1/4 cup Castor sugar
  - 15-20 Walnuts
  - 1/2 Lemon to garnish Orange to garnish Rabdi

METHOD:
  1. Heat ghee in a deep stainless steel handi, add chironji, cashewnuts, raisins and dried coconut and sauté on medium heat for 1-2 minutes.
  2. Add milk, boondi, castor sugar, saffron and ½ tsp cinnamon powder, mix well and cook for 2-3 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. After the mixture comes to a boil, add walnuts and mix well.
  4. Cook for 1-2 minutes, add ¼ cup khoya, cooked basmati rice, mint leaves and remaining khoya.
  5. Squeeze lemon juice into the handi and add remaining cinnamon powder, mix, cover and cook for 4-5 minutes on low heat.
  6. Cut orange into round slices and halve them.
  7. Transfer the biryani onto a serving platter, garnish with rabdi, orange slices and a mint sprig and serve.
